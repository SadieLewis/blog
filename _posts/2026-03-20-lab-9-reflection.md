***Lab 9 Reflection***

This lab wasn't particularly difficult, but it got a bit harder as it went. What I found easiest was the beginning up until after Migrations. I started to struggle with the complex data model section some, it was definitely more challenging to work with than the previous RazorPages website because of how much more stuff there was. That made it more difficult to adjust and add fields like the age field I incorporated into the Student model. I felt like I had to change so much more and tread even more carefully so I didn't mess up all of the data, rather than just the one I was working with. You can see my struggles I had with complex data model in particular on my [Github][github], if you look at my commits. 


That said, I feel like this tutorial will be very helpful in particular with my group's final project. Working with a seed bank, its clients, and volunteers contributing to it will have multiple tasks involved which is where knowing how to use a complex data model should come in handy. It won't be as many different pages and tables, but it will be different entities within a database and at least two different tables to operate with.
