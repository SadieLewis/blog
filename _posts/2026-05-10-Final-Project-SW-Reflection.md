***Final Project SW Reflection***


**Consider the social, environmental, or spiritual need on which you are focusing for this Odyssey project. Do you feel that the approaches to creating social change employed by your community partner are effective ways of creating change in this area? Why or why not?**


    I feel as if the Seed Library Program's approach to creating social change is applicable for a unique type of change. The Seed library educates people on gardening and promotes conservation of arkansas native plants, which helps promote not only environmental prosperity but also social change for those who may be impacted by food insecurity. In permitting people a way to create their own garden with no charge for the seeds, it helps promote that social change for people who are food insecure or live in food deserts by giving them the ability to grow their own food and resources. It benefits environmental prosperity by encouraging planting and teaching those who are engaged about Arkansas native specimens then allowing them to plant those species. I have enjoyed working with the Seed Library as my project's client for this reason, and I have really enjoyed being able to positively impact their cause with the work I have made with my team.
